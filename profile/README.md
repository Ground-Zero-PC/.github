## About Ground Zero

Ground Zero is a survival action shooter that places players in a dangerous and unpredictable environment filled with threats, limited resources, and high-risk encounters. The game focuses on exploration, tactical combat, and survival mechanics as players navigate hostile zones and complete objectives.

Players must gather supplies, manage equipment, and adapt their strategy depending on the situation. Combat requires careful positioning, awareness, and efficient use of resources, as mistakes can quickly lead to failure.

With immersive environments, tension-driven gameplay, and progression through gear and survival systems, Ground Zero offers a focused experience built around realism, decision-making, and continuous challenge.
